About Web Development

Web development is the art and science of creating websites and web applications that are accessible, interactive, and visually appealing on the internet. It involves a combination of coding, design, and problem-solving skills to build user-friendly experiences for people all around the world.

Web Development Roadmap

Front-End Web Development

Front-end development focuses on the visual and interactive aspects of a website. It's all about what users see and interact with in their browsers. This includes everything from the layout, colors, and fonts to buttons, forms, and animations. Technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are the building blocks of front-end development.

Back-End Web Development

Back-end development is the behind-the-scenes work that makes websites function. It involves managing databases, servers, and application logic to ensure that everything runs smoothly. Back-end developers work with programming languages like Python, PHP, and Node.js to create the infrastructure that powers the front-end.
